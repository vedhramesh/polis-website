About POLIS

A small, deliberate society
for future public servants.

We host speakers, build a network, and develop careers — but mostly we build a class of people who will go on to do this work together.

Speaker Events

Off-the-record conversations with senators, generals, ambassadors, and former cabinet officials — roughly fourteen each year.

Networking

Direct introductions to our alumni working on the Hill, in the agencies, at the embassies, and across the consulting and think-tank world.

Career Development

Resume reviews, security clearance walkthroughs, federal-hire workshops, and funded summer fellowships in offices that don’t typically pay.
